mE-Cards

Your quick e-card generator.

Need to quickly send a custom e-card? Well, look no further! This site allows you to create personalized e-cards, each with their own unique URL. Just create a header, a greeting, and/or a footer, pick a background image, and adjust the fonts and borders to your liking. Once you are satisfied with your e-card, copy its unique URL and send it on to your loved one. Sign in to get started - it's completely free!
